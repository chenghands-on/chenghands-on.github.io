This working paper studies endogenous AI adoption in financial markets. In a Kyle-style and mean-field game framework, individually rational adoption of a more precise shared AI signal can synchronize order flow, preserve common forecast errors, amplify price impact, and reduce informed-sector surplus relative to a human-only benchmark.
